Daw, N. D., Niv, Y., & Dayan, P. (2005).
Uncertainty-based competition between prefrontal and dorsolateral striatal
systems for behavioral control.
Nature Neuroscience, 8, 1704-1711.
PMID: 16286932

Key finding: Model-based (goal-directed) and model-free (habitual) systems operate
in parallel with Bayesian arbitration based on uncertainty. Grounds the B computation
as a blend of B_goal (sigmoid of M vs threshold) and B_habit (H-driven).
