Steel, P. & Konig, C. J. (2006).
Integrating theories of motivation.
Academy of Management Review, 31(4), 889-913.

Key finding: Motivation follows hyperbolic discounting M = (E*V)/(1+I*D), not
exponential decay. Grounds the model's hyperbolic M decay: -beta_M * M / (1+M).
