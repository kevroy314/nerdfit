Singh, B., Murphy, A., Maher, C., & Smith, A. E. (2024).
Time to Form a Habit: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis of Health Behaviour Habit Formation and Its Determinants.
Healthcare, 12(23), 2488. DOI: 10.3390/healthcare12232488
URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11641623/

20 studies, 2601 participants. Median time to habit: 59-66 days; mean: 106-154 days; range: 4-335 days.
Simpler behaviors form faster. Morning > evening. Self-selected > assigned. SMD = 0.69.
Refutes the 21-day myth. Recommends 2-5 months for automaticity.
