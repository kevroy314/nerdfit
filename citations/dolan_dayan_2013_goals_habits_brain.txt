Dolan, R. J., & Dayan, P. (2013).
Goals and Habits in the Brain.
Neuron, 80(2), 312-325. DOI: 10.1016/j.neuron.2013.09.007
URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3807793/

Model-based (dorsomedial striatum, prelimbic) = goal-directed. Flexible but expensive.
Model-free (dorsolateral striatum, infralimbic) = habitual. Efficient but inflexible.
Systems operate simultaneously and competitively.
Arbitration depends on relative uncertainty. With experience, model-free dominance increases.
Addiction: dopamine spirals shift to habitual. OCD: habitual overdominance.
