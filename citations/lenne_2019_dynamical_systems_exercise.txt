Lenne, R. L. (2019).
What sustains behavioral changes? A dynamical systems approach to improving theories of change in physical exercise.
Ph.D. dissertation, University of Minnesota. https://hdl.handle.net/11299/209098

Two key processes: automaticity/habit and motivation/satisfaction.
Behavior tracked via Fitbit in Fitbit users who recently increased exercise.
Within-person increases in automaticity associated with increases in exercise time.
Diverging trajectories of automaticity and satisfaction predict maintenance success.
