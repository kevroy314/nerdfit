Kurzban, R., Duckworth, A., Kable, J. W., & Myers, J. (2013).
An opportunity cost model of subjective effort and task performance.
Behavioral and Brain Sciences, 36(6), 661-679.
DOI: 10.1017/S0140525X12003196
PMID: 24304775

Key finding: Effort depletion reflects shifting cost-benefit computation, not literal
resource consumption. Incentives and reframing reverse "depletion." Grounds the
effort cost term as opportunity cost rather than resource drain.
