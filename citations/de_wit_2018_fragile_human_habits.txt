de Wit, S., Kindt, M., Knot, S. L., et al. (2018).
Shifting the balance between goals and habits: Five failures in experimental
habit induction.
Journal of Experimental Psychology: General, 147(7), 1043-1065.
DOI: 10.1037/xge0000402

Key finding: Five experimental attempts to induce outcome-insensitive habits in
humans all FAILED. Human habits may always retain goal-directed character. Grounds
the model's requirement that B always needs minimum M even at high H.
